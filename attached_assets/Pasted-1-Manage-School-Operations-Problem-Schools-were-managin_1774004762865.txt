1. Manage School Operations
Problem

Schools were managing academic, administrative, and communication workflows across disconnected systems (manual records, spreadsheets, offline processes), leading to inefficiencies, data inconsistencies, and poor visibility for stakeholders like teachers, students, and parents.

Research

Conducted stakeholder interviews with administrators, teachers, and parents

Identified key pain points:

Manual attendance and grading processes

Lack of centralized student data

Inefficient communication between school and parents

Competitive analysis of existing school ERP systems revealed overly complex interfaces and low adoption rates

Solution

Designed a centralized School Management System with:

Role-based dashboards (Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent)

Attendance and performance tracking

Fee management and automated reminders

Real-time notifications and communication module

Timetable and exam scheduling system

Impact

Reduced administrative workload by ~40%

Improved parent-teacher communication and engagement

Increased data accuracy and real-time visibility

Faster decision-making with centralized reporting dashboards

2. Madicare (Healthcare Platform)
Problem

Healthcare services were fragmented across hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. Patients faced difficulties in booking appointments, managing prescriptions, accessing doctors across locations, and maintaining medical history.

Research

User interviews with patients, doctors, and pharmacists

Identified major pain points:

Difficulty finding available doctors by location

Lack of centralized medical history

Manual prescription handling

Inefficient appointment rescheduling

Studied digital healthcare platforms to identify gaps in cross-role integration

Solution

Designed Madicare, a multi-role healthcare ecosystem:

Patients

Book appointments based on doctor availability (date/time/location)

Option for home visit consultations with additional charges

Access complete medical history and prescriptions

Doctors

Manage availability and schedules

Add prescriptions directly to patient profiles

Reschedule appointments with automated notifications

Pharmacies

Access prescriptions using unique patient codes

Auto-generate billing from prescribed medicines

Additional Features

Ambulance booking system

Global appointment booking (country/state/city level)

Real-time doctor location availability

Notification system for all updates

Impact

Improved patient experience with seamless healthcare access

Reduced prescription errors through digital records

Increased operational efficiency for doctors and pharmacies

Enabled end-to-end healthcare journey within a single platform

3. HR Portal
Problem

HR teams were managing recruitment, employee data, and internal communication using multiple tools, causing delays, poor candidate experience, and lack of transparency in employee lifecycle management.

Research

Interviews with HR professionals and employees

Key findings:

Manual recruitment tracking

Poor onboarding experience

Lack of employee self-service features

Benchmarked modern HR tools for usability and automation gaps

Solution

Designed a comprehensive HR Management Portal:

Recruitment pipeline with candidate tracking

Employee onboarding and document management

Leave and attendance management system

Performance tracking and appraisal module

Employee self-service dashboard

Internal communication and announcements

Impact

Reduced hiring cycle time significantly

Improved onboarding experience and employee satisfaction

Streamlined HR operations through automation

Increased transparency in performance management

4. Job Search & Professional Networking Platform
Problem

Job seekers struggled with fragmented job discovery, lack of direct communication with recruiters, and no structured way to track applications or build professional visibility.

Research

Conducted user interviews with job seekers and recruiters

Key insights:

Users want better filtering and personalization in job search

Lack of transparency in application status

Need for networking + job platform combined

Competitive analysis of platforms like LinkedIn and Indeed

Solution

Designed a Job Search + Networking Ecosystem:

Core Features

Advanced job search with multi-level filters

Job application tracking dashboard

Direct chat with HR/recruiters

Apply via platform or email templates

Networking System

Connection levels (Level 1 → Platinum tiers)

Gamified points system based on:

Connections

Post engagement (likes/comments)

Rewards: subscription discounts & visibility boost

User Experience Enhancements

Profile creation and continuous updates

Social feed (posts, likes, comments)

AI-driven job and tech recommendations

Global search (jobs, companies, users)

Dashboard with insights and analytics

Notifications and chatbot support

HR/Recruiter Features

Post jobs and manage applications

Filter candidates efficiently

Direct communication with applicants

Impact

Increased user engagement through gamification

Improved job application transparency

Higher recruiter-user interaction rates

Built an all-in-one platform combining jobs + networking + growth
